About Me

Hi, my name is Erik Gomez. I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. I am 28 years old and currently work for AXS LLC as a Client Service Representative, meaning I work directly with entertainment venues and assist in their event builds and troubleshoot and problems they might have. I have a bachelors degree in Psychology and one in Anthropology from the University of California, Riverside.

In my free time I like to stay active, so I try to get some runs in a couple times a week and I enjoy playing soccer on the weekends. I have a four year old baby girl so she occupies a big chunk of my life, which is exciting for me personally. I like to hang out with my friends when time permits and I like to challenge myself, either by learning new things or trying to master things I already know. Any questions feel free to follow me on the socials or shoot me a question.
